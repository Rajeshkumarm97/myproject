The Best Sunglasses

The only "visible" quality test for sunglasses is their fit.
The lenses should fit close enough to the face that only very little
"stray light" can reach the eye from their sides, or from above or below,
but not so close that the eyelashes smear the lenses.
To protect against "stray light" from the sides, the lenses should fit close
enough to the temples and/or merge into broad temple arms or leather blinders.
